Epilogue

Whether in business or personal matters, every problem can
be approached and successfully managed by thinking critically
and applying problem-solving methods. Being skilled in this
process is something that is beneﬁcial to all of us, no matter your
profession or how far along you are in your career. There

are many techniques available when approaching a challenge.
Wendy introduced Dr. Osborne to just a few of them, but each
problem is unique and requires its own set of tools.  Be creative
when approaching life’s challenges, build an analytical toolkit, and
embrace critical thinking in all your endeavors.

There are many ways to gain critical thinking skills: reading a textbook, attending a
lecture, or learning from past experience. In this E-book, you will have an opportunity
to learn how to apply critical thinking skills to business issues through the eyes of
an individual facing many of the same issues as your customers, Dr. George
Osborne.

Through a series of interactive
exercises, you will help
Dr. Osborne analyze his daily
challenges, weigh his options,
and select and implement a
course of action to address
his challenges. Your objective
in working with this E-book
is to develop a personal
approach to applying
critical thinking in your
daily life.

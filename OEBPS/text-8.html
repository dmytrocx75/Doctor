Grayson, had once had success with a small business consulting
group. This group, Spectrum Consultants, had helped him
modernize his local printing business and compete successfully
with a national ﬁrm that opened down the street. Jack happily
shared the number, and George lost no time in calling Spectrum.
Wendy Berman at Spectrum listened sympathetically to George’s
concerns and scheduled a meeting for the next day. George
ﬁnished out the day uneventfully, and drove home feeling ever-so-
slightly lighter with the promise of Wendy’s help.
